yaḥ kaścit subhūte evaṃ vadet tathāgatenānuttarā samyaksaṃbodhir abhisaṃbuddheti [sa vitathaṃ
vadet]1 | nāsti subhūte sa kaścid dharmo yas tathāgatenānuttarā samyaksaṃbodhir abhisaṃbuddhaḥyaḥ
subhūte tathāgatena dharmo ’bhisaṃbodhir abhisaṃbuddhaḥ yaḥ subhūte tathāgatena
dharmo ’bhisaṃbuddhas tatra na satyaṃ na mṛṣāḥ tasmāt tathāgato bhāṣate | sarvadharmā buddhadharmā
iti |sarvadharmā iti subhūte sarve te adharmās tenocyante sarvadharmā iti | ||
