bhagavān āha | tat kiṃ manyase subhūte dvātṛṃśadbhir mahāpuruṣalakṣaṇaiḥ tathāgato ’rhan samyaksaṃbuddho
draṣṭavyaḥ |subhūtir āha | no hīdaṃ bhagavan | tat kasya hetoḥ | yāni tāni bhagavan
dvātṛṃśanmahāpuruṣa lakṣaṇāni tathāgatena bhāṣitāny alakṣaṇāni tathāgatena bhāṣitāni
tasmād ucyaṃte dvātṛṃśanmahāpuruṣalakṣaṇānīti |
