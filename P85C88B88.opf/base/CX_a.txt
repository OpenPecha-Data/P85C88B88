bhagavān āha | tat kiṃ manyase subhūte api nu tathāgatasyaivaṃ bhavati na 1 mayā dharmo deśita
iti | yaḥ subhūte evaṃ vadet tathāgatena dharmo deśita iti | abhyācakṣīta māṃ sa subhūte
asatād udgṛhītena |tat kasya hetor dharmadeśanā dharmadeśaneti subhūte nāsti sa kaścit dharmo
yo dharmadeśanā nāmopalabhyate |
