api tu khalu subhūte yasmin pṛthivīpradeśe ito dharmaparyāyād aṃtaśaś catuṣpadikām api gāthāṃ
bhāṣyeta vā deśyeta vā sa pṛthivīpradeśaś caityabhūto bhavet | sadevamānuṣāsurasya lokasya
kaḥ punar vādaḥ subhūte ya imaṃ dharmaparyāyaṃ dhārayiṣyaṃti parameṇa te āścaryeṇa samanvāgatā
bhaviṣyaṃti | tasmiṃś ca pṛthivīpradeśe śāstā viharaty anyatarānyataro vā gurusthānīyaḥ
