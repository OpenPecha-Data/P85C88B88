tat kiṃ manyase subhūte lakṣaṇasaṃpadā tathāgato draṣṭavyaḥāhaivam etad bhagaval lakṣaṇasaṃpadā
tathāgato draṣṭavyaḥbhagavān āha | sacet punaḥ subhūte lakṣaṇasaṃpadā tathāgato draṣṭavyo
’bhaviṣyad rājāpi cakravartī tathāgato ’bhiviṣyatāha | yathāhaṃ bhagavato bhāsitasyārtham
ājānāmi | na lakṣaṇasaṃpadā tathāgato draṣṭavyaḥ ||atha khalu bhagavāṃs tasyāṃ velāyām
imā gāthā abhāṣataḥ ||

ye māṃ rūpeṇa adrākṣur ye māṃ ghoṣeṇa anvayuḥmithyāprahāṇaprasṛtā na māṃ drakṣyanti te janāḥ
