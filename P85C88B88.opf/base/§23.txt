api tu khalu punaḥ subhūte samaḥ sa dharmo na tatra kiṃcid viṣamas tenocyate ’nuttara samyaksaṃbodhir
iti |nirjīvatvena niḥsatvatvena niṣpudgalatvena samā sānuttarā samyaksaṃbodhiḥ sarvaiḥ
kuśalair dharmair abhisaṃbuddhyate |kuśalā dharmāḥ kuśalā dharmā iti subhūte adharmāś
caiva te tathāgatena bhāṣitās tenocyante kuśalā dharmā iti |
