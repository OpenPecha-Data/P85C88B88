yaś ca khalu punaḥ subhūte bodhisatvo mahāsatvaḥ aprameyāsaṃkhyeyāl lokadhātūn saptaratnaparipūrṇān
kṛtvā dānan dadyād yaś ca kulaputro vā kuladuhitā vā itaḥ prajñāpāramitāyā antaśaś
catuṣpadikam api gāthām udgṛhya dhārayed deśayet paryavāpnuyād [parebhyaś ca vistarena samprakāśayed]1
ayam eva tato bahutaraṃ puṇyaṃ prasavetāprameyam asaṃkhyeyaṃ |kathaṃ ca saṃprakāśayet
yathā na prakāśayet tenocyate saṃprakāśaye iti | ||

tārakā timiraṃ dīpo māyāvaśyāya budbudaḥsupinaṃ vidyud abhraṃ ca evaṃ draṣṭavya saṃskṛtam |
