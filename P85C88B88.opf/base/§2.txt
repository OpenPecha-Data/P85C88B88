khalv āyuṣmān subhūtir utthāyāsanād ekāṃsam uttarāsaṃgaṃ kṛtvā dakṣiṇaṃ jānumaṇdalaṃ
pṛthivyāṃ pratiṣṭhāpya yena bhagavāṃs tenāṃjaliṃ praṇāmya bhagavaṃtam etad avocat |āścaryaṃ
bhagavan yāvad eva tathāgatenārhatā samyaksaṃbuddhena bodhisatvā mahāsatvā anuparigṛhītāḥ
parameṇānugraheṇa1 | yāvad eva tathāgatena bodhisatvāḥ parittāḥ paramayā parindanayā2 |kathaṃ
