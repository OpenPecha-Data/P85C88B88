atha khalv āyuṣmān subhūtiḥ dharmapravegenāśrūṇi prāmuṃcat | pravartayaṃ so ’śrūṇi parimārjya
bhagavaṃtam etad avocat |āścaryaṃ bhagavan | paramāścaryaṃ sugata | yāvad ayaṃ dharmaparyāyaḥ
tathāgatena bhāṣitaḥ | yato me bhagavan | jñānam utpannaṃ na mayā jātv eva dharmaparyāyaḥ
śrutapūrvaḥ |parameṇa te bhagavan | āścaryeṇa samanvāgatā bhaviṣyaṃti ya iha sūtre bhāṣyamāṇe
bhūtasaṃjñām utpādayiṣyaṃti | yā caiṣā bhagavan | bhūtasaṃjñā saivāsaṃjñā tasmāt
tathāgato bhāṣate bhūtasaṃjñā bhūtasaṃjñeti |
