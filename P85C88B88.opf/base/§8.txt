tat kiṃ manyase subhūteya imāṃ tṛsāhasramahāsāhasrāṃ lokadhātuṃ saptaratnapratipūrṇaṃ kṛtvā
dānaṃ dadyāt | tat kiṃ manyase subhūte api nu sa kulaputro vā kuladuhitā vā tato nidānaṃ bahu
puṇyaṃ prasunuyāt |subhūtir āha | bahu bhagavan bahu sugata | sa kulaputro vā kuladuhitā
vā tato nidānaṃ bahu puṇyaṃ prasunuyāt |tat kasya hetoḥ | sa eva bhagavann askandhaḥ |
tasmāt tathāgato bhāṣate puṇyaskandhaḥ askandha itibhagavān āha | yaś ca khalu punaḥ subhūte
kulaputro vā kuladuhitā vā imāṃ tṛsāhasrāmahāsāhasrāṃ lokadhātuṃ saptaratnapratipūrṇaṃ
kṛtvā dānaṃ dadyāt |yaś ceto dharmaparyāyad aṃtaśaś catuṣpadikām api gāthām udgṛhya parebhyo
deśayet saṃprakāśayed ayam eva tatonidānaṃ bahutaraṃ puṇyaṃ prasunuyāt | aprameyam asaṃkhyeyaṃ
|tat kasya hetoḥ | ato nirjātā hi subhūte tathāgatānām anuttarā samyaksaṃbodhiḥ |ato
nirjātāś ca buddhā bhagavaṃtaḥ |tat kasmād dhetoḥ |buddhadharmāḥ buddhadharmā iti subhūte
abuddhadharmāś caiva te |
